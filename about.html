    Get to Know Who We Are

    About Us:

    We are group of friends and associates that have found each other having a similar passion to help the world. Many do not have the time to give, and we understand that. So we have made an effort to set time aside to help every day to which youre support directly contributes to giving to all that are in need. Also, if you are in need, we are here for you.

    Our Cause:

    Founded in May of 2020, we have been working to serve our communities is the best way possible. Here we colaborate with pharmacies, clinics, and other buisnesses to gather the resources and funds to help provide a path to beating Covid-19. We strive to see not only our communities become and stay the most healthy, but the world as well. We recently have been able to collaborate with the worldwide organization AWS to provide server spacefunding. From here we have gotten to see people without the funds and elderly without the support to find comfort in proper medicine and support. We have a desire to not only be gloabal, but maintain a high level of local involvement. These is no task too large or small, and we'd be glad to accept any help you may provide
